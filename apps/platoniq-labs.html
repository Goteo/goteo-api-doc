Platoniq Labs

Developed by: Platoniq

Description

This is an application that shows interconnections between the money raised by the Goteo Platform and the Democratic Online Platform Decidim from the city of Barcelona.

It uses the Sustainable Development Goals to let the user play around projects and invests related with them. At the same time it mixes the relevant meeting places extracted from Decidim where citizen initiatives have been taken place.
